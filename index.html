  • Day 1 (8/5)

    Lesson Summary

    Record an episode of "My Weird School." Draw my life. These will involve drawing and speaking. Play Charades with House Appliances.

    Homework

    Learn about House Appliances.

  • Day 2 (8/6)

    Lesson Summary

    List one food for every letter of the alphabet. Let me ask you complex questions about household appiances. Here is an example of the questions I could ask: https://basicenglishspeaking.com/household-appliance/ Describe your house appliances and home.

    Homework

    Required homework: Learn about cooking. This includes house appliances and foods. Understand the following words: taste (sweet, sour, salty, etc.), ingredients (salt, pepper, chicken, spinach, etc.), cooking equipment (blender, pan, pot, fork, knife, etc.).
    The reason I say "etc." is because I have listed the required words to learn for homework but if the student is interested and has time, they may learn more.

    Just for fun homework: Using home appliances. Use a blender to make a smoothie. Here is a recipe I enjoy: https://www.tasteandtellblog.com/cookbook-of-the-month-review-may-2008/

  • Day 3 (8/7)

    Lesson Summary

    Play hangman with cooking vocabulary. Describe your method of making a smoothie. Describe how to cook a recipe. Play 20 Questions with topics such as "Your Favorite Food" or "Cooking Appliances That You Own."

    Homework

    Read chapter 5 of "My Weird School." Learn about cooking methods (grill, bake, boil, steam, fry) and drinks (milk, wine, juice, tea, lemonade).

  • Day 4 (8/9)

    Lesson Summary

    Discuss what has happened in "My Weird School."

    Homework

    • Learn the months of the year (January, Febuary, March, April, May, June, July, August, September, October, November, December)
    • Learn the seasons (spring, summer, fall, winter)
    • If Vivian is allowed to cook food then here is a recipe for fried rice she should follow: https://www.jessicagavin.com/easy-fried-rice/

    Lesson Summary

    Play games using the months of the year. Discuss the events that occur in each month and season. Categorize activities under each month and season.

    Homework

    • Read chapter 6 of "My Weird School."

    Lesson Summary

    • Learn about the parts of a plant (roots, stems, leaves, flowers) and its life cycle ( http://science4fun.info/life-cycle-of-plants/) https://kera.pbslearningmedia.org/resource/tdc02.sci.life.colt.plantsgrow/from-seed-to-flower/.
